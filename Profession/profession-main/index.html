Secondary Structure

By JPred

Graph Explanation:

Lupas_x: indicates that the coiled coil regions of proteins is predicted by the Lupas algorithm, and x indicates that x amino acids are presented as a set of primitives (x = 14,21,18).

-: the probability of this region being a coiled coil is 50% and below
c: the probability of this region being a coiled coil is 50% to 90%
C: the probability of this region being a coiled coil is 90% and above

JNETCONF： Indicates the confidence level of the prediction (0-9), The higher the value, the higher the confidence level and the more accurate the prediction is.

JNETSOLX： indicates the solvent accessibility, and x indicates the maximum probability of being exposed to the solvent (x = 0,5,25). Take JNETSOLX for example.

-: fragments with a maximum 25% probability of being exposed to the solvent (hydrophilic)
B: fragments with a maximum 25% probability of not being exposed (hydrophobic)

Jnetpred： secondary structure predicted with Jnet neural network, the green part is β-sheet, the red part is helix, and the rest is irregularly folded

JNETHMM： The secondary structure predicted by the implicit Markov (HMM) model, the results are the same as Jnetpred

JNETPSSM： The secondary structure predicted by PSI-BLAST, the results are the same as Jnetpred
